Process: Headline

01. Define

  • Client research
  • Discovery meeting - scope statement
  • Define target audience, objectives and requirements
  • Competitive analysis
  • Define overall effect or feeling to be achieved - creative brief
  • Documents prepared and reviewed with client

02. Concept

  • Design navigation - architecture (site map)
  • Primary and secondary page layout presented in wireframe format
  • Client approval process - navigation/architecture

03. Design

  • Style tiles for look and feel direction (2-3 variations for review)
  • Primary and secondary page layout presented in selected look and feel
  • Client approval process - look and feel

04. Deploy

  • Coding and testing
  • Graphic production and archiving
  • Launch site

05. Manage

  • User accounts creation
  • Internal training- content management system
